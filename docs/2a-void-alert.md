In a high-volume restaurant environment like MK Restaurants, the Void & Exception Alert System is one of the most critical features for protecting the
  business's bottom line.

  Here’s why it is so important for MK's management:


  1. Fraud and Loss Prevention
  "Voiding" a transaction (canceling a bill after it's been printed or partially paid) is a common way for internal theft to occur.
   * The Risk: A staff member could collect cash from a customer, then "void" the transaction in the system and pocket the money. Without an alert system, this
     is very hard to detect unless you manually check every single receipt.
   * The Solution: The system automatically flags any void that happens after a certain time or for a high amount, allowing the President or Manager to
     investigate immediately.


  2. Monitoring Operational Errors
  Sometimes voids aren't about theft; they are about mistakes.
   * The Risk: If one branch has a very high number of voids, it usually means the staff are making many mistakes while taking orders. This leads to wasted food
     and unhappy customers.
   * The Solution: By seeing these "Exceptions" on the dashboard, management can identify which branch needs more training or where the ordering process is
     failing.


  3. Detecting "Ghost" Transactions
  "Exception" alerts also track unusual patterns, such as:
   * Split Bills: Excessive splitting of bills can sometimes be used to hide small thefts.
   * Discount Abuse: If a specific staff member is applying a "Staff Discount" or "Manager Discount" much more often than others, the system will flag it as an
     exception.


  4. Real-Time Accountability
  In the current manual system, a void that happened on Monday might not be noticed until the accounting team reviews the paperwork on Wednesday or Thursday.
   * The Benefit: With the Mobile Dashboard, the President sees the void the same day. When staff know that management is watching "Live," they are much more
     careful and less likely to attempt any dishonest activity.


  5. Protecting Food Costs
  Every voided order usually represents food that was already prepared but not paid for. By reducing voids through better monitoring, MK directly increases its
  profit margin by reducing food waste.

  How it looks in our System:

   1. Red Flags: High-value voids (> 500,000 Kip).
   2. Frequency Alerts: If more than 5 voids happen in one hour.
   3. Staff Tracking: Which user ID performed the void.


  Summary for the President:
  "This system isn't just for seeing sales; it's a security guard in your pocket that protects MK's cash and food from disappearing."
